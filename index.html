Run 2 Brunch

The most important meal of the day is brunch!

San Francisco is the best city in the World to run/bike/hike and then pig out, or at least in the US. The saying: West Coast, best coast is 100% true. Sorry NYC...

The great thing about the Bay Area is that the urban areas are surrounded by protected green (hilly) lands that perfectly complement the vibrant food scene - and we like to take advantage of it, at least once a month!

These are a few of our last adventures:

  • Inner Richmond to Reveille 15.5mi
  • Inner Richmond to Cibo 18.4mi
  • GG Park to Bernal Star 6.6mi

    We always publish the route on Friday and we usually meet at 9am. Don't be scared by the distance, we all have different paces and maintain a no drop policy. EVERYONE gets a pastry at the end!

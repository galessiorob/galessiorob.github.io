About us...

We met 3 years ago running in the Marin headlands and decided to have breakfast together afterwards.

So far, we've repeated the dose at least once a month and our group has grown to 20ish hungry runners that love logging miles and recovering lost calories in San Francisco's best spots.


We have a no-drop policy, and our picks range from fancy-ish sitdown places to the greatest street burritos you'll ever have.

Here our some of our favorite spots:

  • Outerlands
  • Devil's Teeth
  • Reveille
  • Equator Coffee
